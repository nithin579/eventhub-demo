About Event Hub

Welcome to Event Hub, the official event management platform for the College of Engineering Adoor. Our mission is to streamline campus activities, fostering a vibrant community where innovation meets collaboration.

Designed by students, for students, Event Hub serves as a central repository for all workshops, seminars, cultural fests, and technical symposiums. Whether you're looking to showcase your talents, learn new skills, or simply network with peers, Event Hub ensures you never miss out on the action.

Join us in celebrating creativity and technical excellence at CEA!
